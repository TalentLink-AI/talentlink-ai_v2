About Escrow Payments

Escrow provides security for both clients and talents by holding funds in a secure account until work is completed and approved.

  • Secure Payments

    Funds are held securely until milestones are completed and approved.

  • Release Control

    As a client, you control when payments are released based on work completion.

  • Multiple Payment Methods

    Fund your escrow using credit cards, debit cards, or bank transfers.
